Category 01 · Empower & Educate Self

PH-CAT1-003

Community Medication Literacy Workshops

Area of Practice

Community Pharmacy — Rural Outreach

About This Initiative

Launched a monthly medication literacy workshop series across five barangay health centres, teaching patients how to read prescription labels, understand dosage timing, and recognise common drug interactions using plain-language visual aids.

Impact

Over 600 patients attended across 12 sessions in 6 months. Post-workshop surveys showed a 45% improvement in correct medication self-administration and a marked drop in reported adherence errors.

Who Benefited

Elderly patients on chronic polypharmacy regimens, and caregivers of patients with limited health literacy.
